Techno & History

After the fall of the Berlin Wall in 1989, empty buildings became clubs. Tresor (1991) laid the foundation. Shortly after, Ostgut (now Berghain) followed. The underground became culture: gabber events, illegal forest raves, and clubs that shaped music history.
